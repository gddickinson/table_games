# Dragon's Lair — Campaign Guide

## The World of Willowmere

The realm is in peril. The ancient red dragon Scorchfang has awakened and united the Iron Tusk orc tribe under its banner. Undead stir in the ruins of Castle Ravenmoor. Strange lights emanate from a wizard's crypt. Wolves grow bold in the Whispering Woods.

Six heroes gather at the Wandering Wyvern Inn in the hamlet of Willowmere. Their quest: journey across the wilderness, clear the growing threats, and ultimately face the dragon Scorchfang in its volcanic lair.

## Campaign Overview

### Story Phases

**Phase 1 — The Call (Level 5)**
The party meets at Willowmere. The innkeeper Gruff describes the troubles plaguing the region. Two starter quests are available:
- Clear the wolves from the Whispering Woods
- Defeat the ogres blocking Stormcrag Pass

**Phase 2 — Rising Danger (Level 5-6)**
After completing either starter quest, darker threats emerge:
- Undead haunt Castle Ravenmoor
- A wizard's crypt has been disturbed
- (Optional) A hag lurks in the Cursed Swamp

**Phase 3 — Dark Secrets (Level 6-7)**
After clearing the castle or crypt, the party learns that the dragon Scorchfang commands the Iron Tusk orcs. The orc war camp must be dealt with.

**Phase 4 — War Drums (Level 7-8)**
Assault the Iron Tusk orc camp and defeat the War Chief.

**Phase 5 — The Dragon's Lair (Level 8-10)**
The path to Scorchfang's volcanic lair is revealed. The final battle begins.

**Phase 6 — Victory**
Scorchfang is slain. The realm is saved. The heroes return to Willowmere as legends.

## Locations

### Willowmere Hamlet 🏠
The party's base of operations. Rest at the inn, buy equipment, and check the quest board.

**The Wandering Wyvern Inn** — Rest to fully heal (5 gold). Talk to Gruff the innkeeper for rumors and story progression.

**Grimhammer's Smithy** — Buy and sell weapons and armor. Hilda stocks basic gear, with magic items available later.

**Willow's Herbary** — Buy potions, scrolls, and alchemical supplies from the herbalist Willow.

**Quest Board** — Check available quests, track progress, and learn about new threats.

### The Whispering Woods 🌲
*Recommended Level: 5*
A dense forest where wolves have grown unnaturally aggressive. A bandit captain has made camp in a clearing, driving the wolves toward travelers.

**Enemies**: 3 Wolves, 1 Dire Wolf, 3 Bandits, 1 Bandit Captain
**Reward**: 500 XP, 50 gold, chance for basic loot

### Stormcrag Pass ⛰️
*Recommended Level: 5*
A narrow mountain pass blocked by a band of ogres who demand "tribute" from all who pass.

**Enemies**: 3 Ogres
**Reward**: 800 XP, 75 gold, chance for greataxe or armor

### Castle Ravenmoor 🏰
*Recommended Level: 6*
An ancient castle overrun by undead. Three rooms of increasing danger lead to the Wraith Lord on its throne.

**Room 1 — Gate Hall**: 4 Skeletons, 2 Zombies
**Room 2 — Great Hall**: 3 Zombies, 2 Wights
**Room 3 — Throne Room**: 1 Wraith (boss)
**Reward**: 2000 XP, 200 gold, magic weapon chance

### The Wizard's Crypt of Aldric 🗝️
*Recommended Level: 6*
A wizard's tomb guarded by arcane constructs. Puzzles and traps protect ancient knowledge.

**Room 1 — Entry Chamber**: 3 Animated Armor
**Room 2 — Arcane Gallery**: 2 Arcane Guardians (Helmed Horrors)
**Room 3 — Inner Sanctum**: 1 Flesh Golem (boss)
**Reward**: 2500 XP, 250 gold, spell scroll, staff

### Iron Tusk Orc Camp ⚔️
*Recommended Level: 7*
The orc war camp sprawls across an open field. The War Chief must be defeated to break the dragon's army.

**Enemies**: 4 Orcs, 1 Orc Shaman, 1 Orc War Chief (boss)
**Reward**: 3000 XP, 300 gold, rare equipment

### The Cursed Swamp 🌿 (Optional)
*Recommended Level: 6*
A treacherous swamp where a Green Hag has enslaved will-o-wisps to lure travelers to their doom.

**Enemies**: 3 Will-o-Wisps, 1 Green Hag (boss)
**Reward**: 1500 XP, 150 gold, alchemical items

### Scorchfang's Lair 🐉
*Recommended Level: 8+*
The volcanic cave where the ancient red dragon Scorchfang hoards its treasure. Lava pools, crumbling pillars, and the dragon's multiattack make this the ultimate challenge.

**Enemy**: Adult Red Dragon — HP 256, AC 19, Fire Breath (18d6), Multiattack, Legendary Actions
**Reward**: 5000 XP, 1000 gold, campaign victory

## The World Map

The hex map shows the campaign world. Travel between hexes by clicking adjacent tiles. Each hex has terrain that affects travel speed:

| Terrain | Move Cost | Description |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| Plains | 1 | Open grassland, easy travel |
| Road | 1 | Maintained paths between locations |
| Forest | 2 | Dense woodland, slow going |
| Mountain | 3 | Steep terrain, very slow |
| Swamp | 2 | Treacherous wetlands |
| River | — | Impassable except at bridges |

Unexplored hexes are covered in fog of war. Moving reveals adjacent hexes.

## Tips for Success

1. **Rest at Willowmere** between encounters to restore HP and spell slots
2. **Buy healing potions** before every major dungeon
3. **Complete both starter quests** before attempting Castle Ravenmoor or the Crypt
4. **Save your gold** for magic weapons — they make the dragon fight much easier
5. **The Cursed Swamp is optional** but gives good XP for leveling
6. **Stock up on Fire Resistance Potions** before the dragon fight
7. **The dragon is weak to** nothing — but radiant damage bypasses its fire immunity
